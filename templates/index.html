C-19 sound testing contribution

This web application will collect basic demographics, medical history, some voice samples (whilst you read text on the screen), and a few seconds of coughing. Please ensure that when recoding the sounds you are in a quiet environment. We also collect, if allowed, one sample of your location.

The participation in this study is purely voluntary and the details collected will not contain any personally identifiable information.

The entire recording and data collection should take about 5 minutes only. Please keep your device around 20 cm from your face during the recording and perform the recording in a quiet environment. The recording device should not be shared with others before cleaning/sanitation.

By clicking start, you agree to our terms and conditions and provide us the consent to your data solely for research and development purposes.
